about

Will Karmis creates ambient compositions that emerge from the intersection of analog and digital processes. Using reel-to-reel tape, SuperCollider, and software synthesis, he builds sonic environments where generative algorithms collaborate with human intention. Each piece evolves organically from foundational drones and sound grains, developing into non-narrative experiences that prioritize texture over melody, atmosphere over structure.


His practice centers on sound's materiality and the temporal possibilities unique to drone music. These compositions construct immersive environments designed for deep, sustained engagement—spaces where listeners can inhabit sound.
